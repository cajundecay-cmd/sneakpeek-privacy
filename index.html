Privacy Policy for Sneak Peek Lite

Sneak Peek Lite does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

The extension operates entirely locally within the user’s browser and is used solely to temporarily preview custom thumbnails on the YouTube website. No user information, browsing history, or website content is retained or shared.

Sneak Peek Lite does not use analytics, tracking technologies, cookies, or remote servers.
